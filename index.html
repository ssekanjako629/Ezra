Welcome to the Internet Awareness Hub

This platform is created to educate, guide, and empower people on how to use the internet safely, responsibly, and effectively.

In today's digital world, the internet plays an important role in communication, education, business, entertainment, and social interaction. However many people face online dangers such as cyberbullying, hacking, online scams, identity theft and phishing

Common Cyber Threats

Internet threats are dangers and harmful activities that occur online and can effectively affect individuals, businesses, internet users and communitites

However, the internet also exposes users to risks such as hacking, phishing among others and understanding these threats is important because it helps people to protect their own information

Examples of Cyber Threats

  • Phishing Emails
  • Online Scams
  • Hacking
  • Identity Theft
  • Malware
  • Cyberbullying
  • Ransomware

Safety Tips

  • Use strong passwords
  • Do not share personal information
  • Avoid suspicious links
  • Install antivirus software

Online Privacy

Online privacy refers to the protection of personal information shared on the internet. Every time people use social media, websites, emails or online services, they may share sensitive information such as names, passwords, photos and banking details without proper protection, this information can be accessed by hackers, scammers, or unauthorized users
