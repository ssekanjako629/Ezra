Welcome to the Internet Awareness Hub

This platform is created to educate, guide, and empower people on how to use the internet safely, responsibly, and effectively.

In today's digital world, the internet plays an important role in communication, education, business, entertainment, and social interaction. However many people face online dangers such as cyberbullying, hacking, online scams, identity theft and phishing

Common Cyber Threats

Internet threats are dangers and harmful activities that occur online and can effectively affect individuals, businesses, internet users and communitites

However, the internet also exposes users to risks such as hacking, phishing among others and understanding these threats is important because it helps people to protect their own information

Examples of Cyber Threats

  • Phishing Emails: Fake messages or websites used to steal passwords and personal information.
  • Online Scams: Tricking users into sending money or sharing sensitive information.
  • Hacking
  • Identity Theft
  • Malware
  • Cyberbullying
  • Ransomware

Safety Tips

  • Use strong passwords
  • Do not share personal information
  • Avoid suspicious links
  • Install antivirus software

Online Privacy

Online privacy refers to the protection of personal information shared on the internet. Every time people use social media, websites, emails or online services, they may share sensitive information such as names, passwords, photos and banking details without proper protection, this information can be accessed by hackers, scammers, or unauthorized users

Social Media Safety

Social media safety refers to the responsible and secure use of social networking platforms such as Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, WhatsApp, and X (Twitter). Millions of people use social media every day to communicate, share information, and contact with others around the world.

Password Safety

Password safety refers to the practice of creating, managing, and protecting strong passwords to secure online accounts and personal information. Passwords are the first line defence against unauthorized access to emails, social media accounts, banking systems and other online services.

Cyberbullying Awareness

Cyberbullying refers to the use of digital platforms such as social media, messaging apps, and online games to harass, threaten, embarrass, or target another person. Therefore, it is a serious internet issue that can affect anyone, especially young people who spend a lot of time online.

Cyber Security Careers

>Cyber security is a fast growing field that forcuses on protecting computers, networks, and data from cyber threats sucha as hacking, malware, and online attacks. As technology continues to advance, the demand for skilled cyber security professionals is increasing worldwide. These careers are important because they help individuals, companies and governments keep sensitive information safe and secure
